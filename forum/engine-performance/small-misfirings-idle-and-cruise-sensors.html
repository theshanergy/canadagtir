Hey Guys so looks like the source of my problems are as follows

1 - one  of the injectors (cylinder 1) was leaking a small bit of fuel into the electrical connector that controls it. I thought I could smell a bit of fuel but thought nothing of it. changed the injector (apparently DSM 440 injectors fit, which I didn't know) and it seems to be absolutely fine now. 

2 - Changed Cap + Rotor + plug wires.

Running strong!

 Guys smell around your injectors, if you smell fuel it could be the same thing possibly.
