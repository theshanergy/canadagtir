Sentra 1991-1999 with ga16de outside axle head will fit if it's a front axle. At least it's what the FAST is telling me... do you have ABS ? if so it will be a nightmare finding a base model sentra with ABS. As for the shaft it is only fitted to gti-r.

The way i see your problem, the c-clip that hold the shaft inside the head is worn or broken so it slip out. Usually you are not supposed to reuse the c-clip when you work on your axle, like rebuilding them, that's why they come with any boot kit.
