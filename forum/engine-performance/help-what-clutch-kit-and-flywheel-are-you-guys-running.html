The rwd/fwd sr20det clutches are interchangeable, but I have no Idea about the flywheel. If spacing off the crank is different, that could be your problem. If you can contact the guy who did the clutch, ask him if he used the throw out bearing that came with the rwd clutch, They do not interchange from rwd to fwd(they might fit but could be holding the clutch part way in). Could be a rear main seal too, just a few ideas to take into account.

ACT makes GTI-R clutch kits (pressure plate, clutch disc, pilot and release bearings) NS5-HDSS (street disc, rated 370lbs) is what I'm using on my summer Pulsar, and NS5-HDG6 (6 pucks, rated 4xx lbs) on my all season Pulsar. All RWD and AWD SR20DET clutches disc and pressure plates are interchangeable, FWD are smaller. The flywheel is unique to GTIR and I'm not aware of any company who's selling aftermarket flywheels for our cars.

http://www2.advancedclutch.com/products/euro_jdm_apps.aspx
