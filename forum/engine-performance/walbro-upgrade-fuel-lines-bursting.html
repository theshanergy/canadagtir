No problems here, I've put on about 8000kms since the swap. I think the chance of fuel lines bursting really depends on the condition of the car. If it looks like it's been sitting outside (particularly in bad weather) for most of it's lifespan and you've already noticed some metal corrosion / rubber decomposition on the car, then there's definitely a higher chance your hoses could pop. I think most people haven't had any problems, though.
