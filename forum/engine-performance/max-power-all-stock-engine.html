im going to be putting my mines up for sale in a couple of months when i get a stand alone.

my mine's has the speed limiter removed (factory ecu is limited to 180), updated fuel and timng curves, and is set for 1.1 bar about 16psi. there are noticable gains especially up top, smoother powerband, more torque.

you could use an sr20de fwd tranny with lsd with this motor as well.

for fuel, just add an adjustable fuel pressure regulator, if you run the 444cc injectors at 4bar instead of 3bar, i've read its like goign to a 550cc injector. don't quote me on that, i've only read that. but most everyone here runs a 255l walbro pump when turning up the boost from the factory 7psi to 14.5psi.
