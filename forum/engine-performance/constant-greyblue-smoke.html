First off do a Compression Test (equal compression across all cylinders with no more than 14psi high/low across all 4)
This will tell you if the ring lands have failed, if this test fails you will require a rebuild.

Second, remove the air intake tube from the turbo and see if there is any movement of the impeller shaft
This will indicate if the turbo is sloppy and worn, if so, this will require a rebuild or a new turbo (as evidenced by the oil build-up in the charge pipe).

These are the two major factors for blue/grey smoke.
