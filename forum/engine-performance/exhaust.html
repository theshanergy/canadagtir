yup i ran that for about 6 months...i put in a test pipe though instead of gutting the cat cos that platinum is super bad for you and the environment...anyway it works ok but i upped my boost to 1 bar and to be honest if you are going to change ur exhaust do the whole thing 3" right off the turbo or you wont get the performance difference...i changed my exhaust in stages...first the decat pipe with stock everything else...then i had a 3" turbo elbow and downpipe made to mate to the test pipe...that in its self made a nice difference in spool up time, from there i changed the rear exhaust and went under the rear diff...that was the best change by far! the car spools up extreamly fast and the power change is actually noticable...so ya ...if ur going to do anything just do it right if you want the performance benifits...if you just want noise then take the black box off the back and do a straight pipe..lol.
