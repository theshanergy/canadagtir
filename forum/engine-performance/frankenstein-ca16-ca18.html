the 1.8 litre head fits on the 1.6 litre engine. I swapped heads after bending all intake valves after the timing belt tensioner spun its bearings out. The firing ring is smaller on the 1.6l engine compared to the 1.8L.

Double check the mating surface diameters of the cylinder/firing ring and check that the head gasket works to seal any gap. The water and oil galleries line up, so you're good there.

compression will be slightly higher since the 1.6L firing ring has a smaller volume than the 1.8l head.
