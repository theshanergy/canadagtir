there are 2 sensors, one for the pressure gauge and one for the oil light ... I can tell you that 1 kg/cm2 is very low for warm idle ... it should sit at 2 and should rise as rpm rises. That sensor fails often and is not really accurate. As for the light ... maybe the sensor did fail but if IIRC it rarely does.

 

If I were you, I wouldnt drive the car until that problem is fixed. And yes, a minor dent can cause a loss of oil pressure.
