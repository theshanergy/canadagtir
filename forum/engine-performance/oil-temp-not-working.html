What is it you are after?

Do you have a problem with the oil temperature gauge not working?
Do you have a problem with the light in the oil temperature gauge not working?
Are you enquiring about the oil temperature gauge sensor not working so not indicating on the oil temperature gauge?

Responding as best as possible to your questions:

Has anyone else had problems with there oil temp?
I haven't.

Dose anyone know where the oil temp bulb is.
Yes! Oh, you want to know where it is, in the gauge, the left gauge of the three centre dash ones.

Or is there a way to test the sender and or the indicator.
Yep! Use a multimeter on the spade terminals of the sender (attached to the cooler/filter unit at the back of the engine) and also the connector plug, to test continuity to the gauge.
