it may not be the rods. can be any of the following

1. bearings obviously
2. rod bearing surface on rods
3. rod bearing surface on crank
4. combo of any of the above

i'd still take the valve cover off, could be a broken or popped off rocker arm, but it definitely sounds like rod knock to me.

you might be able to get away with just replacing the rod bearings, but i'd get the mains checked too.

what caused it could be a number of things
1. dented pan, so the pick up couldnt get oil
2. oil pump is toast
3. low oil level
4. bad oil fuel soaked or viscosity gone
5. previous owner didn't change oil frequently or keep the oil topped up
6. owner error, driving the car hard without actually doing maintenance items first
