More torque than hp with these engines requires a stroker crank and if your wanting a 'reliable' 400awhp be prepared to spend big bucks. Probably the best way to look at it for reliability: Find someone who has successfully achieved at least 500awhp, mimic their build, run low boost and kiss your fuel economy goodbye lol.... Do NOT get your con rods cryo'ed. Your sacrificing flex for strength and con rods like to flex :)
