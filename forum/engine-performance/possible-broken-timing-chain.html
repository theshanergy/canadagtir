Driving it hard?

Make sure all your boost hoses are tight, spark plugs, wires, cap n rotor changed.
I'd say get a big brake kit, and a walbro fuel pump and you're good.
Clutch watch out after turning up the boost.

Here's what I use
oil - 0w40 Mobil-1 Eurocar formula (I've tried 5w30 burns off, 5w50 is too thick for my liking)
filter - any 240sx filter( i change every 4000km's so it really doesn't matter)
tranny - redline mt90
transfer/rear diff - heavyweight shockproof
spark plugs - ngk iridium bkreix 8
wires, cap,rotor - new oem
fuel pump - walbro 255
rad fluid - just any old with redline water wetter and distilled water
powersteering fluid - dexron3 i think, i just used a turkey baster to change out the fluid

keep an eye on the alternator as it was the first thing for me to go, i changed to new belts when this went.
