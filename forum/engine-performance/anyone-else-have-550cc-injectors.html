keep in mind that a higher boost does not mean more power and might not do the same to the injectors. Boost pressure will be much higher on a more restrictive engine and will not make more power. The one thing to keep in mind which would apply to both is that fuel pressure is increased directly with boost pressure. So a restrictive engine will need more boost and more fuel pressure to do the same power then a less restrictive one.

just food for thoughts.
