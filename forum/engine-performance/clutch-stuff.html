Dyin2Drift wrote:
so got my engine out thats a real &É$%er what fun... k so i got everything dun axles out engine and trans combo and all bolts for the bell housing to engine undone

Well done. Now the first thing to do is unbolt the "Y" bracket at the between the transfer case and the intake and CHUCK it in the bin. It is an over engineered accessory that is not needed unless you are using your R as a rally racer.
Dyin2Drift wrote:
what is the best way to separate the 2 so i can carry on with the clutch.

First of all, have you got all the bolts out. When you remove the starter motor there are three (3) bolts in the area where it comes out from on the engine side of the bell housing, you need to remove them.
Dyin2Drift wrote:
Do i have to separate the gearbox and transfercase to get at the clutch...cheers thanks guys

NO. The gearbox with the clutch bell housing and the transfer case can all be removed in one unit, this will expose the clutch.
