Mishimoto rad option

Hi all,

Installed a new mishimoto rad (MMRAD-SEN-91SR) thought I'd share my experiences. Everything about it fits well (including hoses) except for the rad fan on the driver's side (won't clear the turbo heat shield). I've been running fine with just one fan and I don't care to get a slim fan anytime soon with winter coming. I've taken my car to one track day with it and it worked great but to be fair it was only 10 degrees outside. Not as hardcore as the koyo rad (40mm vs. 53mm) but its around half the price.
