the gt28rs is a bolt on upgrade. There are two styles of compressor HOUSING that I know of, ie two bolt inlet and 3 bolt outlet (similar to gtir) andround inlet and round outlet (rwd sr20 from the s14).

 

Pick your trims for what you want (turbine and compressor).

the stock manifold is good, some people will say it is a restrictive item, however if you don't have the cash for a log/twin scroll exhaust manifold, the stock manifold will do just fine up to about 400-500 hp.

The stock turbine housing style is a T25. So, any turbo that will mate up to the T25 4 bolt square flange will work. T3 turbine housing will not fit directly.

GT28RS

GT2871R

GT2876R

GT3071R (bigger)

GT3076R (bigger yet again)

are all turbos that will bolt right onto the stock manifold and stock T25 5-bolt o2 sensor housing. You will have to manipulate the wastegate bracket or make a new one so that the bracket and rod/actuator clear the turbo and block, but those turbos will work. Then you get to decide what style of compressor housing you want (2-bolt inlet or round) as well as compressor outlet (3 bolt outlet or round).
