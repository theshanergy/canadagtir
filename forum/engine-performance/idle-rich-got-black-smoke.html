with the idle at 950-1000rpm on it's operating temperature (hot) it should be right on 20° and stay there until you rev it higher. it's the ignition timing gauge on your pic

 

you should also check if your MAF is playing within 1-1.5 volts on idle and around 4 volts on full load, a bad MAF can also cause this kind of problem but it's sometimes hard to diagnose it if your don't have a working spare

 

also recheck your spark plug if you have the correct gap. if your problem comes from when you did your tune-up there's a good chance it comes from something you did.
