Check the headgasket by: Firstly and most importantly take a good look at the oil and make sure it doesn't have a cloudy grey/green colour to it (coolant). If so DO NOT run the engine, at all. Coolant in engine oil destroys bearings within a few hours. Check for oil floating in your coolant.  If both of those things check out I'd then pressure wash or brake clean off the entire engine.  Put a water bottle in place of your surge tank and take it for a good hot drive for about 30 mins and see if your coolant system is pressurizing.  If all is well from here compression test your engine (properly, see online manual).
