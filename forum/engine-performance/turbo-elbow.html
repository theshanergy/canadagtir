I've posted more in my thread in the member's car section, but just to give you an update. As this is already a very modified system, Aaron at Weir-Tech and myself chose to make a one piece downpipe from the turbo to the cat. Also, I have no intention of ever having AC on this car, so we're taking advantage of the additional space and have a nice, slow bend. That also allows for the divorced wastegate to tie back into the exhaust even further back. Now this may not work for you, but I want you to know that Aaron has my stock turbo outlet and would be very willing to build anyone a better and direct OEM replacement.
