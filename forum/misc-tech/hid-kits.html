HID kits

I've been following a few threads on HID's from both the UK and Oz forums. From what I've heard, performance for price is at its best with this kit.

This guy is from Australia and this is his opinion:

Quote:
After all the talk on this topic, I decided to take the plunge and bouight a HID conversion kit from Ebay, it is a Hi/Lo version to suit the H4 type globe, colour temperature ordered 4300K, 4500K was supplied , but that was ok because the seller notified me there was no stock, so that was ok, took me about 30 mins like it says in the ad. And what do I think after coming back from a drive, IT IS PERFECT, the cut-off point is much more sharper than what was before, it's dead set scary!! no spill, no stray light hitting oncoming drivers, not a single complaint, I am surprised the kit works that bloody well, so I don't know what everyone is going on about here. but as fas as I can tell there is no need to modify the GTIR to accept this sort of kit. nuff said.
