Disassemble the distributor.  Take the seal to a machine shop or parts store and see if they can match it.  The leaking oil can cover the slots of the optical sensor rotor; I believe that is why it misfires.  Is the code related to cam/crank position?  While you're in there, you should replace the bearing too.  I've seen the bearings wear out and cause the shaft to wobble which causes the rotor plate to contact the optical sensor housing.
