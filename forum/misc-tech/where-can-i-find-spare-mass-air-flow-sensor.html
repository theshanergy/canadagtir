There's only 3 wires used in the 4 pin connector.

1. Orange wire is for the MAF signal 5v
2. White is the MAF ground
3. Orange wire 12v MAF feed.

I used a voltmeter to double check which wire carried the 12v.

You can always refer to this picture:
