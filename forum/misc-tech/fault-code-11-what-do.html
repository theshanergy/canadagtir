Remove your iac valve to clean it with throttle body cleaner or intake cleaner with a old toothbrush. It's not a easy job because you have to remove the 3 bolts holding it with a 5mm Allen wrench behind the intake in a area with almost no space. Never reuse the old gasket, new iac valve gasket part number is 23785-54c00. You will also have 2 connectors to unplug and you'll have to do it before removing the 3 bolts.
