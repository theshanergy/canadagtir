the heater card is fuked. you can fix it temporarily or buy a new card. I ended up getting a new card for around 45$. if you look behind the blower motor, between it and the firewall, you'll see a 2inch wide plug/ white/brown with 4 wires coming out of it. Remove the 2 philips screws and pull the card out. There will be two metal tabs on the card, pop those off with a screw driver. you'll see that one of the exposed metal thingies is burnt out. Clean it off as best as you can and resolder that spot. Just blob a bunch of solder on it. Reinstall and it should work.

else get a new card. p/n 27150-64J60 for the updated card.
