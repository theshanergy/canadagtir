Did you put in a new battery ? if the replcacement battery is new have it checked out at a parts store - it should show about 11.9 to 12 volts to be in perfect shape. Get a volt meter and see what your alternator is putting out.... it should show 13.9 to 14.4 volts when running. If your battery was new, it could be your alternator or alternator charging circuit.
