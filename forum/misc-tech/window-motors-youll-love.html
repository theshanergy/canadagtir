Window Motors you'll love this

So my passenger window wasn't working so i took the door card off and....
[IMG]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y272/davebob/166koz4.gif[/IMG]
the wire was broken and the track pulleys were fucked. So seeing as i had a parts skyline GTST sitting i dug into her and no luck that uses gears. Then out back of my shop i had a 95 parts maxima sitting there dug in and found a dam near perfect match Here's what i did.
[IMG]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y272/davebob/IMG00295-20110615-1502.jpg[/IMG]
[IMG]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y272/davebob/IMG00294-20110615-1502.jpg[/IMG]
i ground off the mounting brackets off the maxima and replaced them with the R ones i chiseled off and lined them up so it would bolt up and tacked them in.
[IMG]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y272/davebob/IMG00296-20110615-1502.jpg[/IMG]
the window does bolt right up with minor ovaling of the hole where the window bolts in and the motor clips in the harness with the stock setup. [IMG]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y272/davebob/window-1.jpg[/IMG]
the 3 motor bolts that bolt to the door do not so u need to drill holes using the r's old motor mount as a template throw some new bolts in and problem solved and it tracks and pulls and pushes really well. You can get the whole setup e.g. motor and track out of any 95 to 99 Nissan maxima. This process took no more than 2 hours. Just hoping to save you guys some money as i couldn't have rebuilt my R without this boards help Thanks and if ya have questions just let me know
