Sorry for the delay.

Got 2 lenses made, but I'm not happy with the quality. I'm forced to heat the plastic too much because it cools quickly when I'm trying to vacform it. This results in a distorted lens that gets brittle, and air pockets can also form in it. Right now I'm building a custom oven that will be a part of the forming assembly. This way i can run MUCH lower temps and have plenty of time to form it. This is turning into a serious manufacturing project!

In the end it will result in a good product I'm hoping, and I can start distributing these for all you guys that want HID projectors or have to comply to DOT or TC standards.

The lens has to be dot approved because it has too meet the beam pattern specifications. The fluting on the glass lens makes the beam pattern. This is a clear lens. Therefore it is the projector or the actual beam emitter. That would have to be approved. But the beam pattern is correct for our roads, and that's what I'm concerned about. Will it pass tech?
