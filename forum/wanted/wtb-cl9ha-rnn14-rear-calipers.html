You need the G20 (p10) calipers, they are closer in e-brake angle to the gtir than the maxima ones.  I used A32 Maxima (Infinity J30) e-brake cables.  They were the longest ones I could find at 67.5" and 66.775"(still a little too short).  They work with just a few mods.  You have to swap all your e-brake cable brackets from your old ones and clamp them on ours.

If you want to spends $$$ you can contact http://www.absco.ca/index.php/en/contact.html.  They are located in Quebec and will make a run of our cables if we send them a sample.  Maybe someone wants to organize a group buy?
