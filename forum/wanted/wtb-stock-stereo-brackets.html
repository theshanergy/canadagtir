thanx anyway guys. i looked yesterday at the g20 and the nx2000 and those brackets arent the right ones. there the same in both cars. i ended up using them tho just with some slight modification lol. i didn't have to change anything at all to do this. the 3 gauges i have in my car just needed to have the screw holes trimmed off and they fit perfectly under the heater controls. my gauges were trimmed when i got my car cause they were originally in the air vent. i bent the brackets and made them fit up top. i drilled one hole on the right side and the left side both screws lined right up with the holes originally there. there was no modification to any panels it all just fit and is very secure. works nice for the screen being in the up position it doesnt get in the way at all. i monitor my gauges while driving and the only thing i need to do now is just look at a different spot but there still very visible.the gauges are just sitting in there not screwed in but they dont come out. they slide a few mm from side to side but you cant really notice unless you move them yourself. it all fit together very nice imo.
