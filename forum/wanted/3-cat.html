Just leave it in, these days the high flow, straight cats don't amount to much flow loss (pressure gain) out of the exhaust.  Their removal used to be an easy 5-20 hp gain.  Now...not so much.  Check the dyno graph from a civic (i don't know the build).

Also, this is a good article.  

http://www.importtuner.com/features/0610impp_catalytic_converter_removal/viewall.html

For me the smelliness was the deciding factor.  GOD DANG did it ever stink with the test pipe, especially since I run really rich.  I got a high flow cat and left it in.  The sound was SLIGHTLY rounded off, but not really any quieter.
