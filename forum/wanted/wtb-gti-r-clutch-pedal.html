Kariba wrote:
I was told the following hydraulic pedals would fit the B13; B15 Sentra, P10 Primera and A32 Maxima.

That's true they work, but for a RHD car it can be very different than what the LHD cars use and can become a nightmare just to fit them. now since you know exactly what pedal box to use you can just go around the junkyard and try to find a clutch master cylinder with the line nozzle pointing straight, upside or the opposite of your brake booster.

Keep the B15 remote clutch fluid reservoir and the clutch master cylinder in stock in case you need to modify the master cylinder you will put in place.
