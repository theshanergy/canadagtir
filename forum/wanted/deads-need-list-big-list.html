nx glove box is totally different. Cluster will not swap without wiring=major headache. Very little nx interior will work properly. The stuff that would be the same, like switches and that jazz, fit, but have different designs. Everything is mirrored because of the RHD.

The rear seat wont fit, the hatch area and gas struts are different. I think the centre console is the same, and the gear shift surround, as well as the shifter. Door panels wont fit, door hinges are different, door latches are the same, door handles, and lock mechanisms are same, steering column switches are on the reverse side and wont work.

Most of the stuff has to be sourced from a gtir.
