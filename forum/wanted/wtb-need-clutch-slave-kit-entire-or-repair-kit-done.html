obviously the best thing to do would be to get a new slave from nissan (which i believe is gtir specific and does not cross reference to any north american models)

or you can try to find a rebuild kit (this assumes that the bore of your old gti-r slave cyl is still in good shape)

the rebuild kit for the gtir is the same part number as the s13 240sx rebuild kit

http://nissan4u.com/parts/info/3062126E25/

another thing you can do is

buy an s13 240sx slave cylinder, take the piston out and put it in the gtir slave

you gotta reuse the old gti-r pushrod though

i just did it yesterday and, combined with a newly installed stainless clutch line, it improved my clutch disengagement considerably
