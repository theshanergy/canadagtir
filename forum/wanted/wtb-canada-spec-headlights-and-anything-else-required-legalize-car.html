well it seems the OP knows more about the legaliseation of his vehicle than the rest of ya :P what imported vehicles need are DTRL's 3rd high mount brake light AS1 or AS2 certified windsheild DOT approved tires OR tires that have load ratings on them Front and rear side marker lights with reflectors Neutral safety switch(only if the car came with one when new) change all your light bulbs as the ones from japan are not bright enough for our standards DOT or E-coded headlights... if they try to say that you need DOT tail lights then tell them to get intouch with Brian kangas, he is a BC CVSE inspector(one of the higher up guys) he has a report from Daniel Stern lighting(they usually do all the DOT testing for car manufactures in NA) which prooved japanese spec(JIS) tail lights to be equal to or better than DOT tail lights, you still need to change the bulb though... exhaust is bit of a touchy one, and really depends on the inspector, id ask around to find out which inspection fascility would give you the least amount of hassle in your area
