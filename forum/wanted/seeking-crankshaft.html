Cranks can still be had from Nissan but they are expensive, last time I looked in the UK they were around £900 but have probabley gone up since then.

Good second hand cranks are also getting rare, spinning bearings seems to be becoming a common failure as the cars get cheaper and are more likely to get bought by people that don't keep on top of changing the oil etc. Good second hand ones in the UK seem to fetch around £200 to £700. Good luck fininding one, as said you can use a rwd SR20DET crank and use an appropiate fly wheel/clutch combination.
