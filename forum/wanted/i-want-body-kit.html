This was custom made. Apparently there was someone who was trying to sell a similar reproduction on ebay for a while but obviously this was designed for a GT/TimeAttack car and thus not for everyone. I think the box flares are excellent and appreciate the old school pure rivet on aesthetic - I really like this car because it was purpose built and the box flares/front diffuser actually serve a purpose (the radiator is actually in the rear of the car and hence the openings in the rear windows).

The issue with 'rice' is the shitty appropriation of track car functionality combined with cheesball graphics, heavy subwoofers,neon and superlame DIY body enhancements - this site makes me laugh and also sometimes very angry http://www.barryboys.co.uk/mx/

The owner of the red car is also on GTIROC but I can't remember the user name. Here are more pictures of the red one: http://speedhunters.com/archive/tags/Pulsar+GTI-R/default.aspx
A cleaner version of these boxflares can be seen on Rishi's car from GTIROC...really nice track car that was just being sold: http://gti-r-us.co.uk/?page_id=99
There is these parts for sale for our car but most of the stuff is a bit lame...the front splitter is not bad and the box flares look clean: http://www.crazyhornet.com/gtir.html#01
