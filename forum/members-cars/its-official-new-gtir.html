Pretty much like the Sunny & Pulsar... It's a Mazda 323 GTR, they made 200 for France and numbered them... They came with the GTX cloth interior Vs. the leather w.red piping... LHD 190hp instead of the Familia 210hp... I've spent hours searching the internet and I've only been able to track down about 10 other numbered cars... I really liked the styling and loved the interior of the Familia's, but I only purchased the car at auction because no one else knew what it was and the price was too good to pass up... Being from Europe it did have some surface rust underneath...

The Pulsar came from the same place the 323 GTR did... About 4 years ago I found an ad online searching for GTiR's in the States... Come to find about 20 minutes away from where I live there was a HUGE barn filled with Skyline's, Pulsar's, Familia's etc... So I went and checked all the cars out but couldn't agree on a price for a black GTiR he had... Randomly this August I was on the search again for a GTiR in the States and after hours I stumbled across a link to an Auction Company website on a forum in California and come to find out all those cars I saw years ago were getting auctioned off in September at No Reserve.

I showed up a day before all the cars were suppose to be auctioned off to check them out again... The next day I went back all Japanese imported cars were pulled from the auction except for the 323 gtr, r32 gts4 shell and an 88 Familia GTX... I was able to purchase a spare hood w.vents and rear bumper w.lip from some idiot for $40(which belonged to a Familia that was pulled)... After emailing back n forth for weeks I was finally able to make a straight trade.
