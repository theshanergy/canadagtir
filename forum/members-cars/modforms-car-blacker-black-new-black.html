Flares are a couple of different universal sets which I will post some further details on after I try to secure a bit of a support deal in case I smash a couple hitting cones!

The wheels are 17x9.5 Rota P45R's 4x100 with a 15MM offset. They are combined with H+R hub-centric spacers with a 20MM in front and 15MM in back. The staggered spacers have helped to dial out understeer and add further grip up front. It is a bit twitchy under brake and a little easier to unbalance but overall there is a dramatic improvement in understeer/oversteer balance.
