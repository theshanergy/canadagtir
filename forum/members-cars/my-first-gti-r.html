My First GTI-R.

Hello. A few months back I purchased a Black 1992 GTI-R with a blown bottom end (#3 piston melted). It's now in the process of getting put back together and thought I would introduct myself with my current build. Looking through old threads I beleive this car used to belong to jonalaps.

Bought the car for $1,200 with the motor pulled and disassembled. It also came with the following goodies:

Mint Garrett  GT2871R A/R .60
Tial QR BOV
Manual Wastegate bleed valve
Exedy Stage 2 Clutch
AFR + Boost Gauge
BC Racing Coilovers
 

NEW stuff for build:

Block has been rebored and crank has been machined
CP 86.5mm pistons
ACL Rod and Main Bearings
Fidanza Aluminum Flywheel
Konig White ET30 15x7.5 +20
OEM Gasket Kit

I attached some photos of how it sits currently. The motor has been assembled to shortblock and should be ready to start moving forward this weekend. Shooting to have the car running within a week, maybe 2.

Sentra/NX2000 B13 Radiator equipped for an SR20DE engine should.  B14 200SX SE-R might too. Koyo lists 1 aluminum rad that apparently fits all of the above; the Koyo 53 mm racing radiator will fit, but 1 fan will have to be omitted.  If you go aftermarket OEM replacement, it may come with an Automatic Trans. cooler and  radiator hose adaptors;the GA16DE engines have smaller radiator inlets and outlets than the SR.  There is also the possiblity of an N15 Pulsar/Lucino with a 25mm core but I haven't tried it personally.
