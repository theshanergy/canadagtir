My long rebuild

Hello guys, here are some pics of my rebuild. Spec: CP 89mm forged pistons with anti-friction coating on skirt and ceramic coating on piston dome. MANLEY turbo tuff forged rods, machined for GT-R 19mm wide bearings. DARTON sleeves. ACL race main and rod bearings( CALICO coated) ARP main stud kit. LUMINI Gas flowed, ported and polished head, polished and volume matched combustion chamber.89 mm combustion chamber, 50cc volume. Bronze valve guides. JUN 272° 11mm lift inlet and exhaust camshafts. JUN titanium retainers. JUN double valve springs. JUN adjustable cam gears. TOMEI 91MM stroke crank. POWER ENTERPRISE 90MM bore, 1.2mm thick steel head gasket. ARP head stud kit. OS GIKEN twin plate clutch and flywheel. PROTECH equal length bottom mount manifold with 48mm runners. Runners length 44cm GARRETT GT35R ball bearing turbocharger,.82 a/r turbine housing. TIAL 44mm wastegate. TIAL 50mm blow off valve CUSTOM 76mm s/s exhaust system with custom made back box . ATP turbo install components, steel braided water and oil lines.). RC ENG 1000cc low impedance top feed fuel Injectors. STR MOTORSPORT modified stock fuel rail. AEROMOTIVE adjustable fuel pressure regulator. Individual coil ignition with 4X LS1 coils. MAGNECOR KV85 ignition leads HALTECH E8 engine management. HALTECH electronic boost controller. BOSCH 040 internal fuel pump. BOSCH 044 (x2) external fuel pumps with swirl pot. MOCAL oil cooler kit and sandwich plate, steel braided hoses. SAMCO water hoses and heather hoses. NISMO oil filter. NISMO thermostat.

NISSAN oil pump.

NISSAN timing chain and tensioner.

NISSAN water pump.

NISSAN gasket kit.
