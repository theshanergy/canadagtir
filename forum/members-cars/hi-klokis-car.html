Hi! Kloki's car

Hey mates,

 

So I bought a 1991 in september and I am very happy I did. It was gambini's. The only modifications done to it are a Deatschwerks 255 fuel pump, front and rear

strut tower bars, a custom straight pipe which I beleive is 2.5" and a Bully stage 2 clutch.

The last owner said there is 180k on the body and 100k on the engine. The body and floor were redone in a few places but it is mint though except for a few dents. 

Not a spot of rust. It's my daily and I will run it this winter. The cold temperatures have arrived so I am running on 5w30 Royal Purple with a K&N hp1003 filter.

I don't have any plans for the car I just want to keep it nice and have fun with it really! I don't have many photos yet so i'll just link the one I got as my avatar so it'll be 

a little bigger. Feel free to ask questions and give me tips! I'm looking foward to talk with you guys.
