vizi0n wrote:
All the stuff in the list for 410bhp?! I would've thought it could make much more power with these parts! I hope you can make some big numbers with the new build

Current spec could be pushed to around 450bhp, but you're then at the limit of the turbo, and anything bigger needs a new manifold.

Also once you get above 500bhp you really need to give serious consideration to lining the block (regardless of what some people will tell you), therefore its a new engine build anyway.

The original plan was just to stick a bigger turbo on it and go for around 500bhp, but I also had my doubts about how long the single plate clutch would last at that sort of power as well as how long the engine would last. So in the end I thought "f*ck it" I might as well build it to a spec where I'm happy with everything and I don't have any niggles in the back of head about reliability, I also wanted to do something unique and as no one else has built a big bore VVL GTi-R in the UK (as far I'm aware) it all seemed to make sense.

The plan with the new build is to push approx. 600bhp (it'll be less but hopefully not too much) with a engine that could potentially be pushed to 800+bhp in the future (in case I get bored again)
