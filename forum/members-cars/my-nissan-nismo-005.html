My Nissan Nismo #005

NISMO PARTS:
- NISMO ID plate on the firewall identifying it as #5
- NISMO 5 point Rollcage
- NISMO Skidplates for engine/tranny, gas tanks, rear diff and brake/fuel lines
- NISMO fornt and rear Strut bars
- NISMO front and rear LSD's
- ARC/NISMO top mount Intercooler
- NISMO Strut/Spring - NISMO Mud Flaps on all 4's
- NISMO Suspension Bushings
- NISMO Engine mount
- NISMO EYE BOLTs for 5 point harnesses on both sides
- Intercooler Sprayer
- EYE bolts to tigh down a full size spare

OTHER PARTS:
- APEX-I intake filter
- HKS Downpipe
- Straight 3" exhaust
- Magnaflow muffler
- 4 brand new KYB AGX adjustable struts
- HKS electronic boost controller EVC 4
- OS Giken Twin Plate clutch
- GTR fuel pump
- ECU RE-mapped and unblocked
- NGK IRIDIUM and NGK RACING cables
- Blitz 80mm boost gauge
- New Nissan Distributor
- The idle and performance seem to indicate there being aftermarket cams but i've never opened it up to check
- NARDI steering wheel
