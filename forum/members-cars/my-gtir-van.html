Good work on the air filter.

The headliner, you'll probably break one or two clips while taking it out. Try to find the pieces that may fall down inot cracks so that they don't end up rattling.

As for the roof rattle, get some thin styrofoam that is really flexible (I don't even thing it's styrofoam more like airy plastic or something. That way you won't get expanding foam dust in 20,000 kms. :p

For the 1 bar loop, you may want to try upgrading your fuel pump first. A Walbro 255 lph pump is a very worth while investment at $110 shipped.

Nice work so far, good luck on the rest!
