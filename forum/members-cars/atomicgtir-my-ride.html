AtomicGTiR - My ride

I needed a winter beater so I picked up my pulsar back in December. OK so it's not really a beater but I had to have it. I'm a total Nissan nut and absolutely love this car. My summer ride is a 2004 Turbo Spec-V but I'll be selling it this spring now that I have this car. It totally brings a new meaning to a fun toy car.

I participate in many autocross/snowcross event and have been drag racing for 6yrs now.

Some of my current mods:
SARD Analyse ECU
ARC Intercooler
3" downpipe, catback & 5zigen pro racer exhaust
HKS EVC3 EBC
Apexi twin chamber blow off valve
GAB strut bars
RS*R lowering springs
Nismo skid plate
Coolingmist Methanol Injection kit
Fastbrake 11" 4 pistons Wilwood caliper brake kit w/HP+ pads
Rota Circuit 8 16" wheels riding on Dunlop Star Spec Z1 205/50/16

So here's the car as of today. I parked it a week ago to finally get the following things done:
-installed Fastbrake 4 piston 11" kit, w/slotted&dimpled rotors, HP+ pads.
-installed new bkr7e spark plugs.
-installed cooling mist water injection kit running straight -40 windshield washer fluid.
-I finally fix my high idle and was revving at 1300 all winter, found a leak in my gas vapor canister so I chucked it. Adjusted idle screw to fine tune to 950 rpm.
-Took out stock torque mounts and applied a shit load of polyurethane to fill them up. The engine isn't trashing around in the engine bay anymore, quite a cheap mod for a $6 tube of polyurethane from Home Depot.
