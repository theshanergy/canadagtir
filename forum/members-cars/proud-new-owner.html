fwiw :) my gtir was bought for the asking price of $7K from a friend of a friend who's involved in rally. I'm a happy camper! Lots of suspension goodies came with it and it's been well maintained and driven on a regular basis including motorsport events.

Some export/import papers that came with my gtir (it had least 2 prior owners in Canada) indicates in 2006 it left Japan for Vancouver BC with a Japanese auction appraisal of 3.5 on a six point scale. (It originated in the Nagano prefecture where it likely got some exercise -- since it arrived it needed new struts and all of the CV joints. A hole for a previously installed fmic is also evident.) A few gtir sold at auctions in Japan lately seem to make as high as a '4' point score with most being at least '3'. The gtir cars that don't sell at auction sometimes seem just not to have AC or there are other issues indicated in a code. The sale price in yen seemed to be around 150,000 yen or aprox $2000cdn for cars like mine with over 100k and under 150K km. Shipping at a guess is well over twice that for a single car. (three gtir in a container to the Regina railhead would be around $5K. est.)

Regarding the JDM car sales market here: a young fellow locally tried bringing in better quality JDM vehicles but it was said he couldn't make a go of it commercially because he could not make enough profit in a market driven by the low end vehicle prices of the jdm examples often imported. People shopped on price point. I am very thankful for the work done by previous owners and the help provided in purchase.
