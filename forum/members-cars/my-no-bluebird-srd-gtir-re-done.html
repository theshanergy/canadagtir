differences:

no more itb's
smaller weaker rods
smaller rod bearings 17mm vs 19mm
different head design
different exhaust manifold, turbo sits closer to block
smaller head studs 11mm vs 12mm
weaker main studs
different main bearings, no oil holes
smaller oem turbo t25
smaller flywheel bolts
hydraulic lifter setup vs solid lifter setup

thats just off the top of my head

he has one advantage though, 
he can do a vvl head swap far easier
