Well time for a little update

- I've change my Blitz DD blow off valve for a Synapse Engineering unit. It responds so much better/quicker, it's day and night. It is vented to atmosphere using the anti-stall kit but I will recirculate later this summer, I just need to get a hold of 1.25" flexible hose
- I've also changed my PCV-killer hoses + BOV actuator hoses for new ones with quick connect couplings.
- Original coolant bottle replaced by a different model (the one I had was leaking coolant on my alternator when going around a corner too fast because of the top vent position)
- Made myself a bracket to support the Z32 MAF, and one for the power transistor
- Changed my gearbox fluid for 1x Redline Lightweight Shockproof and 4x Redline MT-90 ... that drain plug wouldn't move so I had to drain by the reverse switch.
