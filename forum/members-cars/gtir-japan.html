GTiR in Japan

Hey fellow GTiR`s. I`m currently living in Japan. Got this little gem a few weeks ago. Im originally from Abbotsford BC and will be importing it back there next march. Picked it up like this. JIC coils, HKS BOV, HKS filter, Kakimoto muffler. Got a few things planned for the winter. Mainly cosmetic. Smoked sidemarkes, de-badged, removing tint, and polishing lips of wheels with painted gold centres. When I bring it back to Canada I want to do a 3`` exhaust, fmic, and boost up.

Enjoy[img]http://i7.photobucket.com/albums/y256/equilibrio/riders.jpg[/img]

After searching for ages for a car here I came across this little guy. After the initial test drive I was hooked. Driving on the wrong side of the road and a RHD car took a little getting used to but I can manage pretty good now. I live near some pretty cool twisty roads in the mountains and along with bombing down some of Tokyo`s expressways makes driving this car a real blast.
