I wouldn't change only the piston size. If it means bigger pads or bigger rotors then go for it, but a different piston size just throws off the braking bias from front and rear.

I used j30 calipers which do have a bigger piston size but I also used the caliper brackets which accept a much larger pad, and a bigger and thicker rotor (280mm vs 259mm and 28mm thickness vs 22mm)

That said, the braking will probably feel better due to less pedal force, but just not actually be any better and be a lot of work. Try it anyway though if you want and let us know how it works!
