yes, they do. nx2000's and sentra b13's have the same front suspension. just get koni yellow adjustables from a sentra spec V. They're inserts meaning you have to cut out the factory shock and slip them inside. then you can run ground control sleeves. search on the sr20forum for CSK custom shortened koni's. You can also put them in the rear. You reuse the oem housing. The rear's are unique to the gti-r due to the sway bar mounting point and they're a bit longer and more heavily damped for the weight of the awd system. Else you can save yourself the hassle and just get tein's or bc racing coilovers
