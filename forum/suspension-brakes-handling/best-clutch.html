Best clutch

Depending on the BHP & Torque you want to make and what kind of driving you do. If you do alot of standing starts/launches and you will need the paddle.

Helix organic = 320ft/lbs
Helix paddle = 340ft/lbs

OLD AP organic = 320ft/lbs
OLD AP paddle = 340ft/lbs

NEW AP organic = 280ft/lbs
NEW AP paddle = 320ft/lbs

Rps options also available now.
If you want a BIG power clutch the OS Giken dual plate is the one for you.
