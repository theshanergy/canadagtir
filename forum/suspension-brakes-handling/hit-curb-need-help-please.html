The front control arm can be had from an NX.  just keep in mind the ball joints will be nx for the left side and R for the right side (slight difference,  You will definately want a new ball joint in the left side anyway as all the force of the hit was transfered through that into the control arm.

 

The parallel links come in a full set of 4.  but you should know what you are buying before you drop ~300 bucks on a suspension part that you may or may not need.  Chances are that you will be able to pick up a set of stock rear links from someone after the groupbuy goes through.
