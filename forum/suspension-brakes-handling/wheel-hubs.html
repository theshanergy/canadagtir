They look the same dimension wise and should bolt in. They have the same wheel bearings, same brakes, and i believe the same ball joints, but the tie rod ends are different. GTIR take maxima tie rod ends but the difference is in the steering rack thread size and not the hub taper seat.

I compared my gtir hubs to my nx hubs. However, my nx didn't have ABS so you might want to double check that, as that was the only difference I could see. Some nx's came with 4 wheel abs so you might be able to find an exact match.
