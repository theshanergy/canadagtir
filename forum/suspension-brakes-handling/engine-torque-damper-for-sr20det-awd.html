There are 3 support motor mounts on the GTIR and 2 buffer engine mounting rods.

http://nissan4u.com/parts/sunny/el_n14/1991_1/type_49/engine_mechanical/engine_and_transmission_mounting/
[img]http://static.nissan4u.com/epc_img/14172676957362.png[/img]

The cheapest thing to do is pick up some Polyurethane adhesive from your local hardware store ($5-$6) and fill the 2 buffer engine mounting rods. It looks like crap but you can pick up some black stuff if you can find it. No matter as no one can see inside the mounts when they're installed. Take out the rubber part and go to town, I recommend doing multiple layers to ensure proper curing. Start inside, leave it overnight and come back the next day to add more. As a side note, the product does expand when curing so make sure it still fits inside the buffer rod. Doing this will pretty much inhibits all engine sway and allows for much smoother take off, better clutch feel, crisper gear change and awesome launches.
