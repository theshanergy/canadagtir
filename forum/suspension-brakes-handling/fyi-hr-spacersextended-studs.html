Hey Guys...just putting the car back together this week so I won't be able to provide pictures yet.
My Wheels are 17x7.5 with 42 offset(will have to verify this week). The spacers are 15mm. This brings the wheels out much closer to the fender and farther from the suspension. I am running 215/40/17 tires and 215/45/16 R - compounds on my race rims. I am also running Wilwood 6Pistons with 13" rotors which requires more clearance.

Our factory wheel is 14 x 6, 4 x 100, 42 Offset and you can play with offset with the 1010 offset calculator http://www.1010tires.com/WheelOffsetCalculator.asp
I was looking for a slightly wider track to help with cornering and to dial out understeer. There is a great discussion on the GTIROC about using spacers to adjust handling. http://www.gtiroc.com/forums/showthread.php?t=55407&highlight=spacer

Will post up some pics when I get them...
