you can get big brakes for the front from fastbrakes.com anything that fits a 91-94 sentra/nx will fit, i would suggest staying with an 11" kit as you can still run some 15" rims opening a greater selection of performance tires and winter tires.

rear big brakes, there's a post on the forum that shows u how to do it, redrilled sentra spec v rear rotors with a maxima pad carrier

if u dont plan to compete any of the cheap coilover kits bc racing/ksport will do in a 5/4kg spring rate setup

if u need a dual duty spring rate i'd suggest 7/6

if u need track rates.. check out modform's car,  i'd  ask for a revalve and run in the neighborhood of 12-14 front and 10-12 rear

if ur seller gives u the option of switching to swift branded springs i'd go that route.
