OEM slave bored over to 7/8"

So I blew my clutch slave.

I got the correct part numbers off here to rebuild the stock slave... 2 weeks later from nissan

Then I found out my slave was bored out to 7/8" for better pedal feel on my Exedy 6 puck. FAIL

Internet research led me to find this was a common mod to do.. and then led me to the replacement part

A slave cylinder from 88-89 isuzu Truck is 7/8" available from my local Napa next day for $25.00

so I went to my fav machine shop and asked how much they would charge to get the stock slave bored out from 3/4 to 7/8 and honed smooth..

the guy said between 30 and 50 bucks..

result:

you can get better pedal feel on an after market clutch for about $75 depending on your local shop rates.

WIN
