I just discovered that both upper hinges are the same as b13 NX hinges. However, the lower ones are different. They could be modded to fit quite easily but they are different none the less.

The lower ones of course are the ones that wear out.

The NX uses 4 hinges, but there are only 2 different part numbers. That is because on the NX the right lower and left upper are identical and vice versa.

The GTIR has different hinges for the top and bottom and this 4 different part numbers.

I just finished cutting a door hinge apart to find out if one could frankenstien an upper and lower nx hinge together to make a lower GTIR hinge (it would be identical) but the pins are pressed, or welded, or stamped in, (I'm not sure which) And it wouldn't be possible to put it back together unless aftermarket pins were used.

sorry if this ruins the group buy Anthony, but I felt I should let everyone know.

I'm still in for both lower hinges!!!
