engine oil block

hey guys/gals my friends retired and he makes these fairly well . I run one , and pipe mine to a firewall mounted oil filter then to an oil cooler then back to this block. great for freeing up room in the back , and putting your filter where you can get at it, and it still leaves room for a rear mounted alternator. I use the small port on the left for the pressure sender and the small port on the right for the oil temp sender. When i get to my engine i will take some pictures of it mounted . I shopped for a week looking for this part and found one guy from Australia who made one a couple years back. I have them for 129.99 plus shipping  they come with both Viton o-rings  they only fit the GTiR. thanks for reading  Bill
