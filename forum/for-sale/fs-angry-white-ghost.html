FS: Angry White Ghost

OK, here's #2.  The Angry White Ghost.

 

This is a fairly track specific ride, but I have driven it daily for years now.  Not the quietest and smoothest ride around, but it's fast and looks awesome.

 

I don't know what the mileage is, the spedo/odo hasn't worked for a while, and the coolant guage is iffy.  But I fixed the Spedo issue with an Apexi Multi checker.

Now, I'm asking $7000 for this car.  I'm going to be harsh here, I don't what to hear how cheap you can get other GTiR's of that you have a buddy in Japan that can get a better one for less.  If that's your situation, you should go that route.  There are some leaks to address, but as you'll see, there's a chance they'll all get fixed in due time..

 

I have built this car for autocross, and the parts/labour i put into it set it up so for the most part, if I raced, I won.

Professionally wrapped in flat white vinyl by Modform over at TastfulMods.  

The goods: 
- ViPec V44 full wire in stand alone, has an Innovate LM-1 wired in for fueling control and tuning.
- Apexi Multi checker guage
- Tomei ARMS 7960 Turbocharger, good to 400ps
- Spearco front mount intercooler
- HKS twin spark DLI
- Custom exhaust
- Nismo short shifter
- Nismo 2way rear LSD
- Beta spec front LSD
- Every bushing Whiteline makes, minus the steering bush has been done.
- Every bushing SuperPro makes, rear arms have not been done yet.
- Whiteline strut braces, whiteline front subframe brace
- Has Ksport Kontrol Pros. installed less than one year ago.
- Front LCA's have been re-inforced, and run a custom ball joint for more castor
- 7 point bolt in roll cage
- Runs Beta spec doors (18lbs lighter each than Alpha doors) - no power anything!!
- Beta spec rear hatch, 10 lbs lighter 
- Stripped interior, Sparco Sprint seats, pedals,  New Sparco wheel and wheel spacer (Authentic Sparco, not ebay)
- Tomei fuel pump, I have bigger injectors as well that I may include
- Hawk HP+ racing pads in the front, HP street in the back, stops on a dime!!
- 15" Enkei rims with Hankook Ipikes, studded, Rims powder coated Orange
- Setrab oil cooler, installed in oldschool JDM race car fasion! With filter relocate!

PS: FOOT PLATE IN PHOTOS HAS BEEN GONE FOR YEARS.  Sorry.  :-)

Now, this deal will come with a pile of stuff at this price.  It'll include:

- 17" Kosei wheels, white, no tires..
- 15" Work E-wing Negative wheels with Federal 595EVO's, Orange
- Spare short block assembly, has JE postons, needs bearings (Which I have new ACL race series for)
- Another spare long block assembly, so a durable race engine can be assembled without down time again.
- There's probably more, but I can't remember it all.
- Spare good used windscreen.  no cracks.
