FS: JIC Coilovers

Hi there, just bought the car a few weeks ago and these were on it.

The spring rates are "custom" according to when I bought it. I'm not sure what they are - the car handles insane with these but they were much too harsh for my daily driving needs.. I do not plan to track the car. I would guesstimate them to be at about 12kg/mm just based on experience with other coilovers in other cars approx this weight..... great for a car thats intended to be used on the track.. crappy for anything else ;)

They're in good shape spring and damper wise but the pillowball mounts need to be replaced on at least two of the shocks. I'm fairly certain these are reasonably generic and you should be able to get cusco (or some other) upper mounts... I don't know a lot about it.

I can get pics, but they are at a buddies shop.. and I don't have a camera.. so ask if you're serious :)
