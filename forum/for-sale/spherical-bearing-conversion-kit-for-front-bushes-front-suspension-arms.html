spherical bearing conversion kit for front bushes on front suspension arms

Have started a batch of 10 kits.
This kit is a direct fit and allows to convert the front bush on front suspension arms to spherical bearings.
A lot of benefits when installing this kit.
When used with my custom TEFLON anti lift/castor bushes,you will be able to adjust caster angle by changing the position of the arm.

Conversion kit will contain: 
2 X steel inserts 
2X threaded collars 
2X RODOBAL motorsport grade spherical bearings
4X steel spacers
1x steel custom wrench to tighten the threaded collar into the steel inserts 
Price will be $195 for a complete kit. Not bad considering the Powerflex or similar bushes are around $90 and they still allow a lot of movement and geometry changes.
