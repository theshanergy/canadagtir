gearbox case strenghtening collar

Picked up these today...

This strengthening collar is there to prevent gearbox case from cracking and is reliable and highly effective.
Made of 20mm thick 6061 alloy, the collar will literally enclose the housing at its weakest point:The bearing location.

The plate won’t allow the shafts to part out from each other , reducing the stress on the case .

-20mm thick 6061 alloy.

-Precision water jet cut.

-Increases case strength for a bullet proof tranny.

-Welding required.

-Minor hand filing might be required because of case fusion clearances.

Possible goup buy if there is enough interest. Will have price on Monday
