Canadian made GTI-R (54C) Oil relocation block

As seen in the following pictures, this is an oil relocation block for our gtir(54C) engines. It also has a port for actuating vvl solenoids in a ve+t swap. It clears all factory mounts and brackets. With the possible exception of some minor clearancing of the power steering bracket to clear the upper fitting.
I will be making these as they are ordered.
The price will be $150 cad plus shipping (they will be $100 a piece if I can get a group of 10 interested). (this will include -10 fittings for the main oil feed ports a -6 fitting and plug for the vvl port and 2 sets of o-rings.)
Feel free to share in other gtir forums and groups you are part of.
Thanks.
