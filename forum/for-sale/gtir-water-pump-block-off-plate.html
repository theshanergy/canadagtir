gtir water pump block off plate

As per title, specific water pump block off plate for the GTIR-R engine.

8 mm thick 6061 alloy, polished and skimmed for a leak free seal.

Allows removal of the belt driven pump when fitting an electric pump for better cooling.

Std pump is not a monster flowing one, and in inefficient at high rpm.

While a larger pulley helps, it does not solve the issue of cavitation.

Fit an electric pump for improved cooling.
