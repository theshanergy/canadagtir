I have a rare short ratio transmission for sale. It shifted fine but it had a twin plate clutch on it and there is a spot on the input shaft that is slightly worn. It was removed from an imported rally car. I drove it before removal and yes it's short! This is synchronized so its NOT an all out racing dog box with straight cut gears ($60,000!!!).


You can see above the wear is very very minor. Considering the amount of work to remove I didn't want to take a chance on putting the same unit back in!


According to my research here are the ratios:
1st 3.067
2nd 2.095
3rd 1.653
4th 1.272
5th 0.911
Reverse 3.153

Stock ratios are:

2nd 1.850
3rd 1.272
4th 0.954

Reverse 3.266
