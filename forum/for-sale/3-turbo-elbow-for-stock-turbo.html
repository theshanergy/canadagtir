str_motorsport

Stock elbow is very restrictive.This elbow is 3" and allows for a lot more flow and improves turbo spool up.
Even if you have a free flow exhaust,with de cat pipe,the stock elbow will be a huge restriction.
Just look at the internal shape of the stock elbow...there are points where is around 40mm diameter...
There have been cases with a 3071 turbo where the car gained 30bhp just by replacing the elbow with a faster spool up time too.
Better flow,faster spool,reduced EGT
