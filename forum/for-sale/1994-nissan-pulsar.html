1994 Nissan pulsar

1994 Nissan Pulsar GTI-R

113K

$4300

I'm located between Ottawa (Ontario) and Montreal(Quebec). This car is not legal in quebec unfortunately

Decided to sell my pulsar since I’m saving for a house. I’ve had it for a year 1/2 and haven’t had to do any repairs besides the summer tune ups and general maintenance.

Don’t mind the dirty pics. It is the middle of winter.

The good

New pads all around
Kakimoto Catback
Transmission shift very smooth and doesn’t have any grinds.
Doesn’t burn any oil. Never had to top it off between oil changes.
Oil changes always done at 5K with Castrol Syntec (Synthetic)
Front and rear strut tower bars
Basically stock besides the boost upgrade. Came that way from Japan.
Interior is clean which includes all the pulsar floor mats and road flare.
All gauges work
Ac still works
Heated and power folding mirrors.
Windshield is good. No cracks.
Headliner is not fabric, so it doesn’t sag like many of the other pulsars I have seen
Comes with 16” summers rims and tires. I will be keeping my winters for my other car.

The Bad.

Small hole in driver’s seat (pictured)
The clutch is slipping in the higher gears. I still drive it daily and could probably make it last another year. (This is why I have it priced under 5k)
Heater takes a little while in the winter. Probably just the thermostat. The engine does get up to temperature in a normal amount of time, and stays at a steady after that.
Rear fenders have bubbles where they meet the bumper and a larger rust spot at the front. Both pictured. Besides that the car is rust free.
