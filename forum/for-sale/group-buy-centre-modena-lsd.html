[GROUP BUY] Centre Modena LSD

I've been speaking with Frank at Modena for some time about the possibility of getting a custom centre diff done up for our cars.  I was able to get an OZ member, Alex 82, to send Modena a factory centre diff so they had something to work from.  Thank you to Alex for that.

My conversations with Frank have been around finding the right setup to satisfy the GTiR community.  I asked him several questions about different types of differentials that I though would work, and have received the answer that to make this worthwhile, a clutch type/mechanical/ramp and plate etc etc is the best setup for us.  I asked about torsion, Modena is a very experienced company, and Frank explained that in their testing of a Modena torsion centre in an awd setup the car was loose and slow.

On the other hand, the clutch type allowed for a point and drive feel, and resulted in amazing grip on the track.

As well keeping the torque split at 50/50 seems the best option as well.  Those that know, are aware that everything is a compromise.  Modifying the torque split might achieve a better lap time on the track in a purpose built car, but for other drivers will be tail happy, and hard to control aka not ideal for street driving.  Some members also drive in snow or on gravel, where a 50/50 split is ideal.  In the end sticking with Nissan's original split makes sense.

Currently Modena is very busy, so it might be some time before we hear back but I want to know thoughts and opinions and interest.

Only after people speak up for what they want can Frank determine what kind of ramp angles, preload, etc that will suit our needs.  It was my thinking that the majority of owners will want a unit that will perform at the track, but still be street friendly.  You know, allowing tight radius turns to park etc. without making (too) much noise ;).

Updates will follow as I get them.
