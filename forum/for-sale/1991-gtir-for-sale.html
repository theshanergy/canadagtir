fwiw> To a get a rough estimate* of flywheel HP off of the 12.7 sec quarter mile time slip: divide ET by a unit factor of 6.29; then multiply the result by itself three times and then divide that number into the test weight of your vehicle.
formula: HP = {Veh.Weight/([E.T./6.29]^3)}
*factor in formula assumes a 17% powertrain loss i.e. est result above aprox 340hp depending on wt.
