The car has been thoroughly maintained with regular (2,000kms) oil/filter and spark plug (5,000kms changes. I have used this car as a daily driver for the past year and it has never let me down. A compression test was done recently with results in the 155-160 range across all 6 cylinders. The turbo was inspected last fall and I found very minimal shaft play, so for a car with 87,xxx kms it is fairing quite well.

The car boost perfect, stops on a dime with the Brembo brakes and corners like it's on rails (with the KYB suspension, if you get the Hypermax coilovers it will be amazin). It is also VERY reliable, as I have not touched the engine aside from the FMIC. There are some extirior straches and the bumper should be repaired and painted and you could get the rear fenders touched up. For me it was not really and issue because you can hardly see it and it's pretty much a track car. But with a Cut polish it should come out Really nice.

Only trades I will consider is for a Nissan GTi-R, in any color other that black. The trade will be in the $8500 form, since that is what GTi-R's are currently going for. If you have suspension, engine, interoir modifications, I can add items from the option list to even out the trade. The car with EVERYTHING I have for it is well work over 13,000 . Your car must also be in really nice shape and not crazy engine modifications. I need a somewhat reliable car :D.
