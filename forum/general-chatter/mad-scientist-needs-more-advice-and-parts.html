Nistune is getting old and is missing a lot of tuning options compared to today's standards. Get something better like Nismotronic or, even better, a standalone like AEM ems.

If you can, I would also go E85 for fuel to keep intake temperature low

You don't have to worry about increasing HP on a sr20ve-t. Those engines are choked big time by the stock turbo and all you need to go near the 500hp figure are a tubular exhaust manifold with t3 flange, a turbo at least in the gt30 range and bigger injectors. The block internals are also really close to the gti-r crank and rods so no problems there.
