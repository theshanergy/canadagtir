Here is the actual law: 75. (1) Every motor vehicle or motor assisted bicycle shall be equipped with a muffler in good working order and in constant operation to prevent excessive or unusual noise and excessive smoke, and no person shall use a muffler cut-out, straight exhaust, gutted muffler, hollywood muffler, by-pass or similar device upon a motor vehicle or motor assisted bicycle. R.S.O. 1990, c. H.8, s. 75 (1).

That being said, a good performance exhaust shop can install an exhaust that will easily perform just as well, and not get classified as a "Hollywood Muffler". Stay away from the huge tailpipe as it makes you stand out like a sore thumb(your GTI-R already gives you enough attention,lol). I have my stock exhaust on right now, but have spoken to the exhaust shop we deal with at work on a daily basis and can easily go with a 3" turbo back,high flow cat, to a Magnaflow that sits exactly as the oem one and still get a nice sound and some great power increases. Have had a straight through "hollywood" muffler on the girlfriends Mazda for almost 4 years, never had an issue with the cops, but keep the bill from the Mazda dealer where I bought it just in case, as it is somewhat loud. Personally, unless the cop has a db meter,and a valid Ontario mechanic's lic. I don't think he qualifies to be making judgement on loudness.
