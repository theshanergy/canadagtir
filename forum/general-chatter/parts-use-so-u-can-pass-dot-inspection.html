Parts to use so u can pass a dot inspection.

I was thinking in an effort to help guys get there GTiR's thru a b.c inspection i would give a couple a pointers on some parts that are cheap and easy to make work on the R. For D.O.T headlights u can use 88-89 mazda 323 headlights. there is a small gap but they look pretty decent. Look at my car for a visual. When and if u decide to use these headlights u just need to modify the bracket so they bolt to the stock location. USE THE COMPLETE HEADLIGHT ASSEMBALLY FROM THE 323. Or what u can do is order replacements from TYC . They make alot of aftermarket lights. Another option is headlights from a Sunny (Same as a Pulsar For they new guys who don't know.) as long as the Pulsar or Sunny is from a LHD country they will pass. Ebay Italy or Ebay Germany are good options for LHD headlights. Just make sure they are an E code headlight and they will pass inspection here. For side marker lights a nice clean marker can be found on any S13 240sx. They are a cheap nice fitting part and will look stock. u just need to splice into ur parking lights and ur all done. Please feel free to add to this if u have any thoughts.
