I looked at that exact car. They had 2 of them for sale at the time. They were in pretty good shape from what I saw but I remember one had a rusted through exhaust and the other had a super loose drivers door hinge (can't remember which was which). I also seem to recall the interiors being pretty bad on both of them. Might turn out to be a decent car though. Just take your time looking it over and ask to get it on a hoist. Also you need to book in advance to go and see the car (or they will release the hounds on you).
I ended up going private sale. Got mine for $4500 with 98,000km's.

I bought my pulsar from tiger in 2008. At the time they had 5 (2 white and 3 black if I recall) and after having a serious look over all of them along with a mechanic friend I picked the one we thought was in the best shape and I turned out alright. The black one they have now has probably been sitting there at least since I bought mine over 2 years ago. Its probably the last one there for a reason. They do not give you any documentation, warranty, etc. so its really "as is", good luck, you're fully on your own. I would suggest looking elsewhere considering its probably not in the greatest shape and has been sitting so long especially for that price! It might be worth it if you bargain the price down significantly but in my experience bargaining with them will get you things like a free flatbed tow to wherever but not a lower price. Of course my bargaining skills could just be lacking ;)
