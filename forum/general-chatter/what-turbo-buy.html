Stock turbo will flow 320-330bhp on a decent condition engine, topping out at 1.2 bar in terms of being efficient.  Disco spuds only flow to 350bhp so not really worth the upgrade imo.  A GT2871 wil get you to 400-410bhp, although my preference is for a GT3071 which is a much better match between the turbine and compressor stage and will push 450bhp.  After that you've got plenty of options, but you'll need to spend serious money on supporting  mods.
