I chose the GTI-R because I couldn't find a north american for that price with AWD and that power ... stock! If you can get a ride in a GTI-R you will understand... this thread will be useless then, LOL!

Also, the GTI-R is an amazing car for Autocross, the tranny is a little weak for the drag (if power is added). I have had my GTI-R for almost 3 years now and find it reliable for an 18 year old daily driven, sunday autocross and odd track day car. Not necessarily being a mechanic, but in my opinion, you do need to be able to do most of the work on a GTI-R or have deep pockets! Especially if you up the boost and start modifiying it.
