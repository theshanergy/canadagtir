Canadian Tire Motorsport Park on Sunday, September 2 - There will be alligator lapping at noon hour for a 20 minute session. There is no charge. No helmets required. Absolutely no passing - following in a line at speeds no higher than a typical 401 drive. This is during an Ontario Time Attack event so you're welcome to watch the competition. Morning is warm-up - afternoon is competition.

There are huge paddock areas where you can have parking and your own mini-meet. Speed limits on property must be followed. No burnouts, aggressive driving or other stunt driving allowed on the CTMP property. If you haven't seen the Mosport grounds since it's new ownership, it's time to go for a visit. It looks outstanding.
