A free for all timed 'ice dice' was put on by the NASCC at Telford Lake, Leduc, Alberta this Sunday morning in lieu of the previously scheduled year-ender 2 hour sanctioned enduro race. Lots of fun on the ice was had with several classes of race cars and streetcars participating, including 2 local nissan pulsar gtir.
