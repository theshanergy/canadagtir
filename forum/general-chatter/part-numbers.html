Hi mate,

Alot of those part numbers have the jdm sr20det RWD engine setup in them. You can decipher the differences between each engine and their respective parts by the part codes. The RWD engine and parts will have 53J stamped into the part, while the GTiR-specific parts will contain 54C in the part number. As far as I know, this is only to determine between engine and car specific parts, as some parts can be used interchangeably.

I will make a list of the ones you can update with the correct gtir numbers.
