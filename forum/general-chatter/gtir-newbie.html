My advice is look at the article GtiR specifications. It says it all about the RB and RA models. The same thing happened to me when I bought my GtiR, the guy told me it was the rally edition but I checked out the model # and it obviously wasn't. The rally edition did come with some luxury options like power windows and rear wiper but its rare. About the 500 rally editions out there... thats a lie there were only 500 made in the first couple years but they made much more between 91-94. So no, RB editions aren't really all that rare, nore are they all that much better. Good luck on your GtiR though their awesome cars to drive year round, just make sure you don't drive like an old lady!

1. solid lifters, it's going to be noisier than any other sr20, and difficult to adjust, an expensive job
2. injectors are noisy on the gti-r
3. timing chain tensioner - replace it with the latest revised part
4. weight of oil used and age of oil will make the lifters a bit noisier than normal.
