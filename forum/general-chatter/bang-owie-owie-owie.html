Well the short of it is my little rocket is teetering between fixable and written off. The long... I was on my way to work last week with my carpool partner when we got stopped by a train. We sat there for a couple of minutes while the train cleared the crossing but wouldnt yo know when it got to the end of the crossing it started to back up. There were about 16-20 cars in front of me and anothwr 10 and growing behind me. The access to the freeway was about 100m behind me so we decided to go around. We looked out, saw no one, and proceded to do a 3 point turn to get going back the other way. I was stopped across the other lane putting the car into reverse when we got popped. The other guy was further up the line and had the same idea. He didnt brake, no skidmaks, screechas or nothing. Just POP! Anyway, everyone walked away and the guy who hit me drove away. I was found technicaly at fault (boo) got a ticket, had my squirrel rattled from my tree for a bit, and was just lucky no one was hurt. Anyhoo...ill either be getting it fixed or will have a lot of parts avaliable realy soon! lol. The damage was all drivers side from tail light to middle of drivers door. I cant open the door and the drivers glass is shattered. Front fender and a pillar ar okay b pillar might be screwed. Tire was pushed into shock pivoting on driveshaft. Rear subframe looks okay onece I got the tire off and cut the shock out of place. There is spotweld seperation from the rear shock tower and the rear hatch wont close. Either way I will roll in a GTiR again. Single funnest vehicle I have ever driven.
