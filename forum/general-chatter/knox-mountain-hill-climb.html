Knox Mountain Hill Climb

Some of you have seen my NX1600 with the GTi-R engine swap. This past weekend I placed second in SPX 3 class at the Knox Mountain Hill Climb. Had a lot of fun on a course that is more challenging than video portrays. The car that beat me was a 71 Camaro with a twin turbo built motor and a driver who has driven the hill multiple times. This was my first time at the event. There's still a lot of work that needs to be done, but the car is getting there.
