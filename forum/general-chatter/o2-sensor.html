O2 Sensor

I know there have been other o2 sensor threads but i just wanted to offer up this:

http://cgi.ebay.ca/O2-OXYGEN-SENSOR-3W-TITANIA-NISSAN-/200557303448?pt=Motors_Car_Truck_Parts_Accessories&hash=item2eb2259698

I got two of them and they are legit NTK japan made sensors. They are exactly the same in every way as the stock nissan sensor that was on my car, other than the little datsun stamp. Requires the wiring to be crimped or soldered, but you just match up the colours and go.

there is also a UK company that sells an 18mm thread version of this sensor. It's like 66 pounds though plus postage...These are easy to add to an aftermarket system. Just weld in a nissan lug nut, and the sensor threads right in.
