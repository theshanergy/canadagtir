I'm turning 30 in a few months. I've owned a lot of cars. Here's a quick history:

1) 1985 Pontiac Acadian 4 dr hatch... actually shared the car with my older sister as I was only 15 when we bought it.
2) 1978 Chevy Chevette 4 dr hatch... perfect shape, 120 000k when I bought it... wrote it off less than 2 years later.
3) 1991 Mazda 323 LX 2 dr hatch... really fun, quick car. Started my love affair with Mazda and imports in general. Did all the body work on it myself.
4) 2000 Mazda Miata SE... My first and only brand-new car (6 k's when I bought it) Leather, 6 speed, Bose stereo, still probably the best performance car I've ever owned. I miss it.
5) 1976 VW Rabbit... Orange and ugly. I loved it, except I always had to work on it. Not good when it's supposed to be a reliable winter car.
6) 1990 Chevy Corsica LT... 2nd winter car after the Rabbit died. 3.1L V6 was suprisingly strong, but handled like.... a Chevy.
7) 1993 Honda Civic Si Coupe... gf/wife's car. Great reliability/ gas mileage/ performance. Finally sold it after 7 years with 280,000 k's.
8) 2004 Mazda RX-8... a VERY close second for best car I've owned. It was beautiful, but I wasn't willing to drive it in winter, or buy another beater, so I sold it after driving it for 8 months.
9) 1993 Nissan Altima GXE... car totally suprised me. Good power (4 cyl), good handling, fun 5 speed, nice interior... just rusty.
10) 2003 Toyota Matrix XRS... 180hp, 6 speed, but still not a great performer. Made in Canada, and all the squeaks prove it. About to become my wife's car, heh heh.
11) 1993 Nissan Pulsar GTIR... arriving in the next week.

Thanks for reading, writing brought back many memories.
