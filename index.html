Canada GTiR

Nissan Pulsar GTiR Canada forum

Canada GTiR is a website for Nissan Pulsar GTi-R / GTiR enthusiasts in or around Canada. In my search for the perfect Pulsar GTiR I came across lots of very good information - but none for Canada, and especially not any for BC (my home Province), so I decided to make canadagtir.com, where you can find all the answers to your Pulsar GTiR questions and maybe give some helpful hints to some new or potential Nissan Pulsar owners.

Here you will find a guide to importing and insuring a Pulsar GTiR from Japan to Canada as well as any modifications needed to get it past a provincial inspection.

Hope to hear from some fellow Canadian Pulsar GTiR owners!
